Circles Audio Visualization

Drop an MP3 file to play it, or switch to the mic input. Requires Chrome for WebGL.

1. The number of rings should be determined by the sounds detected

2. The speed of the rings is determined by the frequency. Higher frequencies travel faster and lower frequencies travel slower.

3. The width/thickness of the ring is also determined by the frequency. Higher frequencies result in thin rings and lower frequencies result in thicker rings.

4. Colors of rings should be random.

Sample Music: "Notorious" by Malaa
